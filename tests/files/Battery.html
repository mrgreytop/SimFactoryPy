Batteries are the most efficient vehicle fuel that is not radioactive. They cannot be used to generate power.

Batteries are used to fuel Drones.

AWESOME Sink[edit | edit source]

 Battery can be sunk into the AWESOME Sink to yield 4 712 points (513 points).

History[edit | edit source]

  • Patch 0.4.0.0: Introduced new recipe, made the ingredients of the old recipe an alternate recipe
  • Patch 0.1.20: Energy value increased from 1,000 MJ to 6,000 MJ
  • Patch 0.1.14: Made officially available
